Information

Our unique, award winning Kalahari skin care range is inspired by an extraordinary journey that started in the vast open plains of the Kalahari Desert in Africa. More than a cutting-edge natural treatment; it is an experience of Africa itself…

The science behind it all

The highest percentage of effective botanics with select Phyto African certified ingredients, combined with ingredients from renowned international laboratories. Our products are paraben, sulphate, MIT & artificial colorant free. We are proudly FDA and EU compliant…

Our Products

Click on products and select your category, to view our complete range of skincare, beauty and lifestyle body products to compliment and enhance your everyday Kalahari skin care routine. Enjoy that special “Salon” feeling at home, keeping you and your body revitalised throughout the year…

Kalahari in the Media

Local and International Award winning beauty and lifestyle products. Innovative, intuitive and scientifically formulated to suit all skin types – acclaimed as a truly unique locally sourced product for a global market. Connect with us in the media…
